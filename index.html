Memory Enhancing Button Game

Hi there traveller! Why don't you sit here and test your memory?
Select your level and press Start to play.
On Level 1, you have two miss chances and the speed is normal.
On Level 2, you have one miss chance, and it speeds up a little.
On Level 3, you cannot miss, and it gets FAST.
While playing, you can use the cheat and heal yourself up (get extra miss chances).
And listen to a song to relax your mind. (Press -listen music, then -start.)

Good days to you Sirs and Misses. Have a great memory!
